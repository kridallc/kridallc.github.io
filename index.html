About Krida LLC

Science Meets
Software

Krida LLC was founded in 2022 with a mission to build applications that combine technical depth with practical, engaging design.

Led by a founder with a research background spanning Penn State, Sandia National Laboratories, University of Michigan, and Colorado School of Mines, Krida brings scientific rigor to every product.

Our work spans spatial computing on Apple Vision Pro, cross-platform consumer apps, and science education tools — each built to solve a real problem.

Spatial Computing
One of a small number of independent developers with a published native visionOS app on the App Store.
Research Background
Founded by a materials scientist with postdoctoral experience at top US research institutions.
Multi-Platform
Building across visionOS, iOS, and cross-platform with Swift, Flutter, Firebase, and RealityKit.
Full Product Lifecycle
From concept and UI/UX design through development, testing, and App Store launch.
