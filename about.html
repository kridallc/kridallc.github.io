In His Own Words

Research trains you to ask hard questions and follow them wherever they lead. That same instinct led to Krida — a conviction that the depth and rigor of academic work could be channeled into building technology products from scratch, and a quiet confidence that the skills would follow. Four years later, with four apps shipped across iOS, Android, and Apple Vision Pro, and more in development, that conviction has held.
