Built-In Structures
Six crystals. Every major system.

Each structure is fully interactive — rotate, zoom, expand into a supercell, and measure from any angle.

C
Diamond
Cubic

The ultimate covalent network — each carbon bonded to four others in a tetrahedral lattice of extraordinary hardness.

Au
Gold
Face-Centered Cubic

The archetypal FCC metal — closest-packed atoms arranged in a structure that gives gold its unique malleability.

NaCl
Halite (Rock Salt)
Cubic

The classic ionic crystal — alternating sodium and chloride ions in an interpenetrating FCC lattice.

CaTiO₃
Perovskite
Orthorhombic

One of the most technologically important structures — the template for solar cells, superconductors, and ferroelectrics.

SiO₂
Quartz
Trigonal

Silicon and oxygen in a helical lattice — the structure behind piezoelectricity and one of Earth's most abundant minerals.

TiO₂
Rutile
Tetragonal

Titanium dioxide in its most stable form — studied for photocatalysis, solar energy, and semiconductor applications.

Features
Built for precision exploration.
🔭

Full 3D Interaction

Rotate, zoom, and pan from any angle. See how atoms pack and bond from perspectives impossible with flat diagrams or physical models.

🌐

Immersive Space Mode

Place your crystal structure in your actual room with Apple Vision Pro's spatial computing. Walk around a room-scale unit cell — literally.

📐

Bond Measurement

Tap any two atoms to measure bond distance and angles in real time. Ground your visual exploration in the actual crystallographic data.

🧱

Supercell Expansion

Expand any structure from 1×1×1 up to 5×5×5 repetitions. Visualize the periodic lattice and understand long-range crystalline order.

⚛️

Element Info Panels

Tap any atom to see element data — symbol, atomic number, radius, and role in the structure. Exploration and reference in one gesture.

🎬

Auto-Spin Animation

Enable continuous rotation for presentations, lectures, and demos. Your crystal structure becomes a living exhibit — no hands required.

CIF Import

Bring your own structures.

✦ Included · No purchase required

CIF (Crystallographic Information File) is the universal standard for crystal structure data. CIF Import is fully included with CrystalRoom — load any structure from databases like the ICSD, COD, or your own research files, with no additional unlock required.

The full app experience is available to all users. Import, explore, measure, and expand any valid CIF structure alongside the six built-in crystals.
