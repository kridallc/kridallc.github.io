Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone and cancel from there. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for any App Store purchase.
How do I reset my password?
If you signed in with Apple or Google, password recovery is handled by those services. If you have an email/password account, use the password reset page.
Is Maths Dash available on Android?
Currently Maths Dash is iOS only. Android support may be added in a future release.
What is the free trial?
All new users receive a one-week free trial before any charges apply. Cancel before the trial ends and you won't be billed.
Does Maths Dash collect my personal data?
No. Maths Dash only uses Apple or Google Sign-In for authentication. No personal data is stored on external servers. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
