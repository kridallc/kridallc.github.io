How to Play
One rule. Zero mercy.

The entire control scheme fits in a single sentence. What happens after you start — that's a different story.

01

Drag to Move

Place your finger anywhere and drag. Your player follows. That's the only control you'll ever need.

02

Don't Get Hit

Homing enemies track your position constantly. Spinning blades, electric fields, crushing walls, and deadly pits fill the arena. All at once.

03

Adapt or Die

Every 20 seconds the rules change. Three new modifiers shift the game — fog descends, blades accelerate, enemies clump, your speed doubles. Every run is different.
